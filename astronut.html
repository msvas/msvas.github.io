Astronut

Astronut is a multiplayer game that puts you in control of a squirrel astronaut!

Description
Race against your friends and jump from planet to planet so you can get faster to the finish line.
But be careful, there are black holes that can desintegrate the squirrel!

How to play
Player 1: arrow keys
Player 2: W A S D
